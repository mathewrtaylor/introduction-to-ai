# Workshop Plan: Deepseek Discovery - Unleash Your AI Co-pilot

## Workshop Metadata

- **Duration:** 90 Minutes
- **Format:** Interactive Presentation + Guided Demos + Team Game
- **Participants:** Up to 30
- **Materials Needed:**
    - Projector and screen
    - Laptop with a modern web browser
    - Stable internet connection
    - Participants should have their own laptops/devices and a [Deepseek account](https://chat.deepseek.com/)
    - (Optional) A slide deck
    - (Optional) Prizes for the game winners (e.g., company swag, gift cards)

## Workshop Agenda

1.  **Part 1: The Introduction - Meet Deepseek** (15 mins)
2.  **Part 2: Live Demo - Seeing is Believing** (20 mins)
3.  **Part 3: The Hands-On Workshop Game: "Prompt Quest"** (40 mins)
4.  **Part 4: Wrap-Up & Call to Action** (10 mins)

---

## Part 1: The Introduction - Meet Deepseek (15 mins)

**Goal:** Set the stage, build excitement, and explain the "what" and "why."

### The Hook: "AI in Your Pocket" (0-5 mins)

> "Good morning, everyone! Raise your hand if you've used an AI tool in the last week. (Pause). Now, keep your hand up if you feel you're using it to its full potential. (Pause). Today, we're going to change that. We're not just talking about AI in the abstract; we're getting our hands dirty with a specific, powerful, and free tool called **Deepseek**. By the end of this session, you'll have a new co-pilot for your daily work and life."

### High-Level Overview & Unique Strengths (5-15 mins)

**What is Deepseek?**
> "Deepseek is a Large Language Model (LLM) from a Chinese company. Think of it as an incredibly knowledgeable and versatile digital assistant that understands and generates human-like text."

**Key Capabilities (The "What it can do"):**

- **Conversation & Q&A:** Like a super-powered search engine you can talk to.
- **Content Creation:** Write emails, reports, blog posts, social media content.
- **Code Generation & Debugging:** For our technical folks, it's a brilliant coding partner.
- **Analysis & Summarization:** Feed it a long article or a set of data and get the key takeaways.
- **Creative Brainstorming:** Generate ideas for projects, marketing campaigns, or even team events.
- **File Processing:** It can read and process text from uploaded files (PDFs, Word Docs, PowerPoints, Excel sheets, and images with text). *This is a huge one to highlight!*

**What Makes Deepseek Unique? (The "Why Deepseek?"):**

1.  **It's Completely Free:** No subscription fees, no tiered plans. This is its biggest headline feature.
2.  **Massive 128K Context Window:** It can remember and work with *very* long conversations or documents without losing the plot.
3.  **Strong Reasoning & Math Skills:** Consistently ranked as one of the top models for logical reasoning and complex mathematics.
4.  **Web Search (Optional):** It has an optional web search function you can toggle on for real-time information.
5.  **Multilingual Proficiency:** Excellent at understanding and generating content in numerous languages.

## Part 2: Live Demo - Seeing is Believing (20 mins)

**Goal:** Demonstrate core capabilities with clear, relatable examples. Show, don't just tell.

### Demo 1: The Advanced Assistant (File Upload & Analysis)

- **Prompt:** "Team, I've been sent this market research report as a PDF, but I don't have time to read all 20 pages. Let's get Deepseek to help."
- **Action:** Upload a (non-confidential) PDF. Use the prompt:
    > "Please analyze this report. Summarize the three key takeaways for a senior manager. Then, identify the primary market challenge mentioned and suggest two potential strategies to address it."
- **Takeaway for Audience:** "See how it didn't just summarize? It followed my complex instruction to analyze and suggest. This is a game-changer for processing long documents."

### Demo 2: The Creative Co-pilot (Structured Brainstorming)

- **Prompt:** "We need to name a new internal project focused on sustainability. Let's get some creative ideas."
- **Action:** Use the prompt:
    > "Brainstorm 10 creative names for an internal corporate sustainability project. The names should be catchy, positive, and reflect themes of green energy and efficiency. For each name, provide a one-sentence explanation."
- **Takeaway for Audience:** "Notice how it didn't just give a list. It provided structured, explained ideas based on my specific criteria. This is the power of a good prompt."

### Demo 3: The Technical Partner (Code & Logic)

- **Prompt:** (Aimed at the more technical members) "I have a JSON data set of customer orders, but I need a Python script to find the top 5 customers by total revenue."
- **Action:** Paste a sample JSON structure and the prompt:
    > "Given this JSON data structure, write a Python script to parse it and output the top 5 customers by total revenue spent. Include comments in the code."
- **Takeaway for Audience:** "Even if you're not a coder, this demonstrates its logical prowess. It can write, debug, and explain code in dozens of programming languages."

## Part 3: The Hands-On Workshop Game: "Prompt Quest" (40 mins)

**Goal:** Make learning fun, collaborative, and competitive. This is where they build muscle memory.

### Game Setup

- Divide participants into teams of 3-4 people.
- Create a shared slide or document with 4 "Quest Stations."
- Teams must complete each challenge using Deepseek. They will copy-paste their prompt *and* Deepseek's best response into a shared document (e.g., a Google Doc) under their team name.
- The first team to complete all quests correctly wins!

### The Quests

#### Quest 1: The Document Detective

- **Challenge:** "We've uploaded a short, public-domain story to the shared drive. Your quest: Upload the document to Deepseek and ask it:"
    > **"Who is the culprit, and what were the three key pieces of evidence that led to that conclusion?"**
- **Skill Practiced:** File Upload & Information Extraction.

#### Quest 2: The Email Alchemist

- **Challenge:** "Your manager sent you this messy draft: *'hey team meeting tomorrow 10 am need everyone there to talk about the q3 budget and the new project timeline also dont forget the client reports k thanks.'* Your quest:"
    > **"Rephrase this draft into a professional and clear email suitable for senior leadership."**
- **Skill Practiced:** Tone Shifting & Professional Writing.

#### Quest 3: The Idea Generator

- **Challenge:** "The office is feeling dull. Your quest:"
    > **"Brainstorm 5 creative and low-cost ideas for a 15-minute team energizer activity. For each idea, provide a list of any needed simple materials."**
- **Skill Practiced:** Creative Brainstorming & Structured Output.

#### Quest 4: The Logic Puzzle

- **Challenge:** "Solve this classic puzzle:"
    > **"'You have a 3-gallon jug and a 5-gallon jug. How can you measure out exactly 4 gallons of water?' Ask Deepseek to not only give the answer but to explain the step-by-step reasoning as if it were teaching a beginner."**
- **Skill Practiced:** Complex Reasoning & Explanation.

### Game Flow & Debrief

- **(15 mins) Game Play & Facilitator Support:** Circulate the room! Help teams that are stuck. Announce progress to build excitement.
- **(5 mins) Game Debrief & Winner Announcement:** Congratulate the winning team. Showcase one or two excellent prompt/response pairs.

## Part 4: Wrap-Up & Call to Action (10 mins)

**Goal:** Reinforce key learnings and ensure adoption continues.

- **Recap the 3 Key Takeaways:**
    1.  **Deepseek is a powerful, free co-pilot** for thinking, creating, and analyzing.
    2.  **The magic is in the prompt.** Be specific, provide context, and ask for the format you want.
    3.  **It's a skill, not a magic trick.** The more you use it, the better you'll get.
- **Your Challenge:** "For the next week, I challenge each of you to use Deepseek for **one real-world task**."
- **Resources:** Provide a link to a shared resource doc with:
    - Links to Deepseek.
    - A "Prompt Crafting Cheat Sheet".
    - The slides from today's workshop.
- **Q&A:** Open the floor for final questions.
