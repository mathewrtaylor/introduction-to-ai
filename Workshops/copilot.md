# 🧠 Workshop Title: “Unlocking AI Superpowers with Copilot”

## 🎯 Workshop Objectives
- **Familiarity**: Help participants understand what Copilot is and how it works.
- **Literacy**: Build foundational knowledge of LLMs and AI concepts.
- **Comfort**: Encourage daily use through hands-on practice and fun challenges.

---

## 🗓️ Workshop Structure (2–2.5 hours)

### 1. Welcome & Icebreaker (10 mins)
- Quick poll: “Have you used an AI tool before?”
- Fun question: “If AI could do one chore for you forever, what would it be?”

---

### 2. Intro to Copilot & LLMs (20 mins)

#### 🧬 What is Copilot?
- A conversational AI built on large language models (LLMs).
- Created by Microsoft to help with productivity, creativity, learning, and more.
- Available across platforms: Windows, Mac, Web, iOS, Android, Edge, GroupMe, and WhatsApp.

#### 🧠 What’s an LLM?
- A type of AI trained on massive amounts of text to understand and generate human-like language.
- Can summarize, translate, brainstorm, write code, and more.

#### 🌟 What Makes Copilot Unique?
- **Image generation & editing**
- **Voice interaction**
- **Vision (screen and camera understanding)**
- **Pages for long-form collaboration**
- **Memory (when enabled) for personalization**
- **Actions for web-based tasks**
- **Daily updates, quizzes, and podcasts**
- **Available in Microsoft 365 apps like Word, Excel, PowerPoint**

---

### 3. Live Demo Prompts (20 mins)

Use these to show off Copilot’s versatility:

#### ✍️ Creativity
- “Write a limerick about our team’s Monday meetings.”
- “Generate a logo idea for a fictional coffee brand.”

#### 📊 Productivity
- “Summarize this document in 3 bullet points.” *(Upload a sample)*
- “Create a table comparing three project management tools.”

#### 🧠 Learning
- “Explain quantum computing like I’m 12.”
- “Quiz me on European history.”

#### 🖼️ Visuals
- “Generate an image of a futuristic city skyline at sunset.”
- “Edit this image to add a glowing effect.” *(Upload one)*

#### 🎙️ Voice & Vision *(if available)*
- “Tap the mic and ask: What’s this object?” *(Use camera or screen share)*

---

### 4. Hands-On Game: “Copilot Quest” (45 mins)

Split into teams of 3–5. Each team gets a **quest card** with 3 challenges. They must use Copilot to solve them. First team to complete all wins!

#### 🧩 Sample Quest Card
1. **Creative Spark**: Ask Copilot to write a short poem about your team’s favorite snack.
2. **Data Dive**: Upload a CSV and ask Copilot to visualize the data.
3. **AI Detective**: Ask Copilot to explain the difference between phishing and spoofing.

🏆 **Bonus Round**: Each team creates their own challenge for another team to solve using Copilot.

---

### 5. Wrap-Up & Reflection (15 mins)
- Share favorite discoveries or funny outputs.
- Ask: “What’s one way you’ll use Copilot this week?”
- Provide a cheat sheet of prompt ideas for daily use.

---

## 🧾 Materials You’ll Need
- Projector or screen for live demos
- Devices for participants (laptops or phones with Copilot access)
- Printed or digital quest cards
- Sample files for upload (CSV, image, document)
- Cheat sheet handout (prompt ideas + Copilot features)

---

## 💡 Optional Add-Ons
- **Leaderboard** for ongoing Copilot challenges post-workshop
- **Copilot Prompt Wall** in the office for sharing cool uses
- **Follow-up session** for advanced use cases (e.g., coding, automation)
