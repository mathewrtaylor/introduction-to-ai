## 2️⃣ What is ChatGPT? (10 mins)

### ✳️ Big Picture

- ChatGPT is a **Large Language Model** (LLM), developed by **OpenAI**
- Based on **GPT-4** (or **GPT-4o**)
- Trained on a huge variety of text data
- It generates responses by predicting the next word, not by "knowing" facts

### 📌 Unique Characteristics

- Can summarize, translate, write code, brainstorm, and more
- Can follow instructions, mimic tone, and format outputs
- Optionally has **tools and memory** (depending on the plan)
- Can sometimes "hallucinate" — sounds right, but may be wrong!

> 💡 *Think of ChatGPT like a really fast, very polite intern who tries hard but doesn’t always get it right on the first try.*

## 3️⃣ Capabilities Overview (10 mins)

Demonstrate these live using quick, visual prompts:

### 🔍 Summarization
```text
Summarize this article in plain English: [paste article]
```

### 🌍 Translation & Tone
```text
Translate to French and make it sound formal:
"Hi there! Just checking in about the meeting."
```

### 💻 Coding Helper
```text
Write a Python script that scrapes a webpage and saves the results to a CSV
```

### 🎨 Creative Writing
```text
Write a limerick about a cat who learns to code
```

### 🖼️ (Optional) Image Generation
```text
Generate a realistic image of a robot making a sandwich
```

## 4️⃣ Prompting Principles (10 mins)

Teach with contrasting examples:

| Principle       | Example |
|----------------|---------|
| Be Specific     | ✅ "Give me 3 bullet points summarizing this report for my boss" vs ❌ "Summarize this" |
| Give Role/Context | ✅ "Act as a legal assistant. Review this contract and flag potential concerns" |
| Provide Format  | ✅ "List pros and cons in a table" |
| Iterate         | ✅ "Now simplify that to a 5th grade reading level" |

Let participants suggest vague prompts, then improve them together.

## 6️⃣ Group Game Workshop: 🎲 Prompt-a-Palooza (35 mins)

### 👥 Setup

- Teams of 4–6
- One device per team (ChatGPT access)
- You act as **Game Master**
- Score tracked on a whiteboard or shared doc

### 🕹️ Game Rounds

| Round | Task |
|-------|------|
| 1. Creative | Generate the opening paragraph of a children’s book called “The Vacuum That Ate Mars” |
| 2. Business | Create a professional email declining a meeting but proposing an alternative time |
| 3. Analysis | Paste in a sample spreadsheet and ask ChatGPT to suggest 3 insights |
| 4. Trivia | Design a 3-question quiz on the history of AI for grade 6 students |
| 5. Wildcard | Use ChatGPT to help design a silly team mascot and pitch it in a haiku |

🔊 End each round with:
- **1-minute team share-out**
- Funniest, cleverest, or most impressive gets points

🏆 Prizes:
- Mini trophy
- Candy
- "LLM Master" printable certificate

## 📄 Optional Handout: 1-Pager Cheat Sheet

### What is ChatGPT?
> A conversational AI that helps with writing, research, brainstorming, coding, and more.

### Prompt Tips
✅ Be specific✅ Provide role/context✅ Ask for format (bullets, table, summary)✅ Iterate/improve your output❌ Avoid vague or single-word prompts

### Example Prompts
- “Summarize this Slack thread in 3 bullets for my manager.”
- “Act as a resume coach. Improve this bullet point.”
- “Turn this plain text list into a formatted table.”
- “Translate this email into French, keep it casual.”
- “Write a tweet based on this paragraph.”
