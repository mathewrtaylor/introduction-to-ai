# Workshop: Meet Mistral—Your AI Partner

## Overview
A hands-on, engaging workshop for teams to build familiarity with Mistral, raise AI literacy, and increase comfort using LLMs as daily tools.

---

## Workshop Outline

### 1. Introduction: Meet Mistral (15-20 min)
**Goal:** Demystify LLMs, introduce Mistral, and highlight unique features.

**Content:**
- What is an LLM? (Brief, jargon-free explanation)
- Who is Mistral? (Creation, capabilities, and what makes it unique)
- Why Mistral? (Use cases relevant to their work/lives)
- Addressing Common Concerns: (Privacy, accuracy, limitations)

**Delivery:**
- Use analogies (e.g., “Mistral is like a super-powered research assistant, brainstorming partner, and translator all in one”).
- Show a short, fun video or meme about AI to lighten the mood.

---

### 2. Demo: Mistral in Action (20-25 min)
**Goal:** Showcase Mistral’s capabilities with live demos.

**Demo Prompts:**
| Task Type      | Example Prompt |
|----------------|----------------|
| Creativity     | “Write a haiku about our team’s mission.” |
| Productivity   | “Summarize this email chain and suggest a polite response.” |
| Technical      | “Explain how a blockchain works to a 10-year-old.” |
| Multilingual   | “Translate this paragraph into French, then summarize it in English.” |
| Unique Features| “What’s a unique fact about Mistral that most people don’t know?” |

**Tips:**
- Use real-world examples from their industry if possible.
- Encourage questions and reactions after each demo.

---

### 3. Workshop: AI Scavenger Hunt (30-40 min)
**Goal:** Hands-on practice, teamwork, and fun!

**Game Setup:**
- Divide into small teams (3-5 people).
- Each team gets a “scavenger hunt” list of tasks to complete using Mistral.

**Sample Tasks:**
| Task            | Example Prompt |
|-----------------|----------------|
| Creativity      | “Write a team cheer using our company values.” |
| Analysis        | “Analyze this short article and list 3 key takeaways.” |
| Problem-Solving | “Brainstorm 5 ways to improve our team meetings.” |
| Technical       | “Explain the difference between AI and machine learning in 3 sentences.” |
| Fun             | “Tell a joke about our industry.” |

**Scoring:**
- Teams earn points for creativity, accuracy, and speed.
- Bonus points for using advanced features (e.g., multilingual, code generation).

**Debrief:**
- Teams share their favorite responses.
- Discuss what worked, what was surprising, and what was challenging.

---

### 4. Wrap-Up & Next Steps (10-15 min)
**Goal:** Reinforce learning, address lingering questions, and encourage ongoing use.

**Content:**
- Recap: Key takeaways from the session.
- Q&A: Open floor for any questions.
- Resources: Share a one-pager with prompt tips, use cases, and safety guidelines.
- Challenge: Encourage them to use Mistral for one work task this week and share results in a follow-up meeting.

---

## Logistics & Tips
- **Tech Setup:** Ensure everyone has access to Mistral (via web or API) before the session.
- **Facilitation:** Keep energy high, use humor, and celebrate “aha” moments.
- **Adaptability:** Be ready to adjust pace based on the group’s comfort level.

---

## Why This Works
- **Engagement:** Games and teamwork make learning fun and memorable.
- **Inclusivity:** Tasks cater to all skill levels.
- **Practicality:** Focuses on real-world applications, not just theory.
