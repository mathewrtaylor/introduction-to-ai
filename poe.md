# Workshop Outline: "Getting to Know Poe"

**Duration:** 2-3 Hours

## 1. Introduction (30 mins)
- **Welcome & Icebreaker**: Quick introductions and a fun icebreaker related to AI (e.g., "What would you ask an AI if you could only ask one question?").
- **What is Poe?**: 
  - Briefly explain Poe as a platform for interacting with language models.
  - Introduce the concept of Large Language Models (LLMs) in simple terms.
- **Capabilities Overview**:
  - Discuss various capabilities: text generation, summarization, question answering, creative writing, etc.
  - Highlight unique features of Poe (e.g., conversation style, adaptability, etc.).

## 2. Demo Prompts (30 mins)
- **Live Demonstration**:
  - Show live examples of different prompts:
    - Creative writing (e.g., storytelling)
    - Summarization (e.g., summarizing a lengthy article)
    - Q&A (e.g., answering specific business-related questions)
    - Brainstorming (e.g., generating ideas for a project)
- **Audience Interaction**: 
  - Invite participants to suggest prompts for you to try live.

## 3. Workshop Activity: "AI Challenge Game" (1 hour)
- **Setup**:
  - Divide participants into small teams (4-5 members).
  - Provide each team with a different challenge or prompt to solve using Poe.
- **Challenges Examples**:
  - **Creative Writing Challenge**: Write a short story incorporating three random words.
  - **Business Strategy**: Generate a marketing plan for a fictional product.
  - **Problem-Solving**: Use Poe to brainstorm solutions for a common workplace issue.
- **Team Collaboration**: 
  - Teams discuss and create their prompts, then use Poe to generate results.
- **Presentation**: 
  - Each team presents their findings or creations to the group.

## 4. Wrap-Up Discussion (30 mins)
- **Share Experiences**: Participants share what they learned and any surprising outputs from Poe.
- **Discuss Applications**: Explore how they might use LLMs in their daily work and personal lives.
- **Feedback Session**: Collect feedback on the workshop and suggestions for future sessions.

## 5. Closing (10 mins)
- **Resources**: Provide additional reading materials or links for further learning about AI and LLMs.
- **Q&A**: Open the floor for any final questions.

---

## Tips for Engagement:
- **Interactive Elements**: Use tools to allow real-time feedback (e.g., live polls).
- **Gamification**: Consider adding a points system for teams based on creativity, problem-solving, and presentation skills.
- **Visual Aids**: Use slides or visual tools to illustrate key concepts and make the session more dynamic.
